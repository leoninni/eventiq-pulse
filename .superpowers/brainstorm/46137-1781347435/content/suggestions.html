3 High-Impact Improvements for EventIQ Pulse

Ranked by combined VC signal strength + customer utility. Pick which to build first.

A

Candidate Match Score + Open Roles

Add 3–4 hardcoded open roles (e.g. "Senior ML Engineer", "Backend Infra") and show a per-candidate match score on every row in the Candidates view — mirroring what the Recommendations tab does for events. The candidate detail panel would show which role they match and why (skill overlap).

VC angle
AI that produces a concrete hiring outcome, not just analytics. Makes the product defensible — the match model is trained on your data.
Customer angle
"Which of these 247 people should I actually call?" is the real job-to-be-done. Right now the product can't answer it.
B

Pipeline Conversion Funnel

Replace the donut chart on Overview with a per-event conversion funnel: Attendees → Opt-ins → Contacted → Interviewed → Offers. Show drop-off % at each stage and highlight which event had the best funnel efficiency. The donut moves to a secondary position or disappears.

VC angle
Unit economics made visual. An investor can immediately see the TAM, retention, and conversion thesis in one chart.
Customer angle
A Head of Talent can show the CFO exactly why a €8k sponsorship was worth it — or wasn't. Justifies the budget conversation.
C

ATS Integration Flow (Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby)

Make the "Add to ATS" button in the candidate drawer actually work in the demo. Show a modal with ATS logos, a connection state, and a mock sync success. The candidate row gets a small "Synced to Greenhouse" badge after. Current "coming soon" toast kills credibility in any live demo.

VC angle
Integration = distribution channel + switching costs. Shows enterprise GTM awareness. Ashby/Greenhouse partnerships are a real moat.
Customer angle
Every recruiter lives in their ATS. Without this, EventIQ is a second tool they have to maintain in parallel — a dealbreaker at procurement.
Also worth noting (lower effort, quick wins):
• The email template hardcodes "Syntara Engineering" — should be your company name for any real demo
• Bulk actions on the candidate list (select multiple → change status / export) feels table-stakes for recruiters
• The Recommendations shortlist has no CTA after shortlisting — what happens next? A "Request Budget Approval" button would close the loop
